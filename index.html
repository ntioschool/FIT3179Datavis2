New Vehicle registrations to Metro station boardings

This map displays the vehicles registered per suburb to the passengers boarding metro stations from 2020 to 2022. We can see that the availability of metro stations in the area reduce the number of new vehicles registered in the surrounding suburb. This is likely due to the availability of public transport within the area.
